--- Manually build ---
- Probably more adequate for large (re-)builds.

    sphinx-build -b docs docs/_build/html

--- Build once ---
- Probably more adequate for small new clean builds.

    sphinx-build -E docs docs/_build/html

--- Auto-build ---
- See changes online in real time.

sphinx-autobuild docs docs/_build/html --open-browser
